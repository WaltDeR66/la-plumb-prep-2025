1-Hour Study Plan: LSPC Administration & Authority

Minutes 0-5: Introduction to the LSPC

Core Adoption: Understand that the Department of Health and Hospitals, Office of Public Health, officially adopts Part XIV (Plumbing) of the Sanitary Code, State of Louisiana (LAC 51:XIV).
Terminology: Recognize that "Louisiana State Plumbing Code" (LSPC), "this code," and "this Part" are all synonymous and refer to the same document. This is crucial for understanding any text related to the code.
Minutes 5-15: Authority and Legal Framework

Primary Authority: Identify R.S. 36:258(B) as the foundational law granting authority for the sanitary code.
Specific Provisions: Note that more detailed regulations are found within Chapters 1 and 4 of Title 40 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes. These statutes provide the specific powers and guidelines.
Minutes 15-25: Promulgation and Amendment History

Promulgation Basis: Understand the code is officially promulgated according to R.S. 40:4(A)(7) and R.S. 40:5 (specifically citing subsections (2), (3), (7), (9), (16), (17), (20)).
Key Dates:
Original Promulgation: June 2002 (Louisiana Register LR 28:1383)
Latest Amendment Mentioned: November 2012 (Louisiana Register LR 38:2795)
